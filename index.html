Our Story

Where Africa Meets Dubai

Heaven of Africa was founded with one mission: to bring the vibrant, diverse and deeply nourishing foods of Africa to the multicultural city of Dubai.

From Nigerian egusi and West African palm oil to Ethiopian berbere spice and East African moringa — we source directly from trusted farmers and producers across the continent.
